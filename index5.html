How do I get started on a web design and development project with you?
Getting started on a web design and development project with me is simple, and it all starts with a good chat. I want to know everything about your business, including your goals for the website, your ideal customer, and your personal preferences. Once we've set the terms and agreed on the details, we can start the magic and bring your vision to life. And don't worry, I'll make sure to keep you informed every step of the way!
How long does it take to complete a website project?
The timeline for completing a website project can vary based on several factors. Some of the main factors include my current workload, how quickly you can provide feedback and approval, and the complexity of the project. Additionally, other factors such as the complexity of the website and the need for custom features or functionalities may also impact the project timeline. Don't worry, I'll work closely with you to establish a realistic timeline and keep you updated every step of the way. But a good starting point is 3 months.
How much does it cost to hire you for a web design and development project?
As every project is unique and requires a tailored approach, I don't have a fixed rate. The cost will depend on several factors, including the scope of work, timeline, level of complexity, and specific features or functionalities you want me to implement. However, as a rough estimate, a typical project starts at around 3000 EUR. I always provide detailed proposals and transparent pricing to ensure there are no surprises along the way.
Can you help me with the content creation for my website?
Of course! I can help you with the content creation for your website. We have two options: we can either rely on the magic of AI softwares that generate tons of content variations, or we can tap into the brain of a wordsmith who will provide us with the perfect wording for your website. Either way, we'll make sure your website speaks the language of success!
Do you offer website support and maintenance after the project is complete?
Absolutely! I'm here to support you long after the project is complete. For starters, every contract includes 4 weeks of free support and 1 year warranty. And as for website maintenance, I'll be real with you - if it ain't broke, don't fix it. If your website truly needs it, I'll be happy to take care of it. But if not, I won't be the one to push it on you.
What kind of content management system (CMS) do you use for websites?
When it comes to content management, I don't believe in a one-size-fits-all approach. That's why I've teamed up with the experts at SCDesign.ro to provide you with a fully customized CMS that's tailored to your specific needs. Our CMS is built from scratch using the latest back-end technologies, ensuring that it's always up-to-date and ready to handle any special requests. And don't worry if you're not tech-savvy – I'll personally provide you with training to help you navigate the system. Plus, if you have any questions or need further assistance, I'm always here to help!
Will the wesite be SEO friendly?
Forget about SEO being an afterthought, it's one of the main stars in my web design and development show. I take great care in making sure your website is not just SEO friendly, but SEO irresistible. I mix on-page optimization tactics with clean and correct source code to make sure your website not only gets attention, but keeps it long-term. So, rest assured that your website will be ready to climb those search engine rankings and become the star of the show.
Do you provide web hosting services or do I need to find my own?
Let's leave it to the experts. I don't offer hosting myself, but I'll gladly help you find the best hosting service for your website. Plus, as a cherry on top, every project comes with one year of paid hosting to get you started on the right foot.
What happens if I want to make changes to my website after the project is complete?
I've got you covered! You'll have access to the content management system (CMS) that comes with every project, so updating things like your contact info or social media links will be a breeze. Plus, I'll provide you with personalized training and support to ensure you're comfortable making changes on your own. And if you ever need help with anything else, just give me a shout – I'm always here to assist you.
